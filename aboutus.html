About us

Austin Pharmaceuticals started in 1996. Austin is a GMP and WHO certified Pharmaceutical manufacturing unit. It is leading pharma company in India. We are reputed manufacturers and suppliers of an effective grade of medicines from last 22 years.

With in-depth knowledge and vast experience in this industry, we have come up with an optimum grade of Pharmaceutical Tablet, Pharmaceutical Capsules and Pharmaceutical Syrups. Our proficient and experienced chemical experts make use of excellent quality chemical compounds and advanced processing technology to attain unmatched quality in our offered grade of medicines. Highly effective and reliable in curing various diseases, these medicines are the prime choice of the clients. Our offered medicines are highly effective and don’t have any side effect. These medicines are widely acclaimed and demanded in the market for their unmatched quality, excellent packaging, accurate composition, long shelf life, high effectiveness, quick action and no side effects.
